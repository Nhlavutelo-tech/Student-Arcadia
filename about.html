About Student Arcadia

Who We Are

Student Arcadia is a dedicated company that bridges the gap between landlords and students, creating a seamless housing experience for both. We specialize in helping landlords prepare, accredit, and manage student accommodations that meet the standards required by NSFAS and other educational institutions.

Our Mission

To create a trusted network that supports both landlords and students by providing quality housing, reliable services, and a smooth accreditation process.

Our Vision

To become the leading partner in student accommodation solutions, empowering landlords and enhancing the student living experience across South Africa.
